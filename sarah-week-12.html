Baby Size

Your baby is about the size of a plum this week.

Baby Development

At 12 weeks, your baby is developing essential features, including tiny limbs that are starting to move. The face is becoming more defined, with the formation of the eyes, ears, and nose. This week also marks the development of the digestive system as your baby starts to produce a hormone called insulin.

Mom's Symptoms and Tips

  • Nausea: Try ginger tea or crackers to help ease nausea throughout the day.
  • Fatigue: Rest whenever possible, and consider small, frequent naps to recharge.
  • Mood swings: Practice relaxation techniques such as deep breathing or meditation.

Weekly Self-Care Tip

Take a moment each day to connect with your baby; try talking, reading, or even singing to them.

Nutrition Focus

This week, focus on getting enough folic acid, which is crucial for your baby's neural development.'

Foods to Avoid

  • Raw or undercooked seafood, as it can contain harmful bacteria.
  • Deli meats, which may pose a risk of listeria infection.
  • Certain fish high in mercury, which can affect your baby's nervous system.

Hydration Tip

Aim for at least 8 to 10 glasses of water daily to stay properly hydrated, especially as your blood volume increases.

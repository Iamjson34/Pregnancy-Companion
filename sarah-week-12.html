👶 Your Baby This Week Lime

This week, your baby is forming vital organs, including the heart, and beginning to develop the facial features. The limbs are lengthening and even tiny fingers and toes are starting to take shape. By the end of this week, your baby's bones are starting to develop, laying the groundwork for a stronger future.

What To Expect This Week 🤱

Nausea

Try eating small, frequent meals throughout the day to help settle your stomach.

Fatigue

Rest whenever you can, even if it's just for a few moments. Take naps if needed!

Breast tenderness

Wear a supportive bra to help ease discomfort.

💡 This Week's Tip

Make time for a soothing evening routine, such as taking a warm bath or practicing gentle yoga.

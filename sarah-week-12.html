👶 Your Baby This Week

Lime

At 12 weeks, your baby is starting to develop the ability to make complex movements, and tiny tooth buds are beginning to form. Their facial features are becoming more distinct, and they are even starting to practice swallowing. By now, the baby is also developing their unique set of fingerprints!

What To Expect This Week 🤱

Nausea ⚠️

Try to eat small, frequent meals throughout the day to help manage feelings of nausea.

Fatigue ⚠️

Make sure to rest when you can, and consider taking short naps to recharge your energy.

Mood swings ⚠️

Take time for mindfulness or light exercise to help stabilize your emotions.

💡 This Week's Tip

Consider taking a few moments for deep-breathing exercises each day to help you relax.

🥦 Nutrition Focus: Folic Acid – Essential for baby's neural tube development during the first trimester.
⚠️ Foods to Avoid:

Raw or undercooked meat – Risk of foodborne illness.

Certain fish (like shark, swordfish, and king mackerel) – High mercury levels can be harmful.

Unpasteurized dairy products – Risk of harmful bacteria.

Your Baby This Week 👶

Lime

This week, your baby is developing fine features as their face becomes more defined. The heart is now fully developed and is pumping blood effectively. Tiny buds that will become arms and legs are starting to form, and your baby's movements will soon be more noticeable!

What To Expect This Week 🤱

Fatigue

Try to rest whenever you can and don’t hesitate to ask for help!

Nausea

Keep small snacks like crackers on hand to help manage feelings of nausea.

Mood Swings

Take deep breaths and engage in activities you love to lift your spirits.

💡 This Week's Tip

Consider taking a short walk each day to boost your mood and energy levels.

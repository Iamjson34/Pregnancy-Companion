Your Baby This Week 👶

Size: Lime

Your baby is about the size of a lime this week! The vital organs are beginning to take shape, and the baby's facial features are becoming more distinct. Arms and legs are continuing to grow, making movement possible, though you may not feel it yet.

What To Expect This Week 🤱

Nausea ⚠️

Try ginger tea or crackers to help ease the discomfort.

Fatigue ⚠️

Listen to your body and take short naps when needed.

Frequent urination ⚠️

Stay hydrated but try to limit drinks before bedtime.

💡 This Week's Tip

Take a moment each day to practice deep breathing or light stretching to relax your body and mind.

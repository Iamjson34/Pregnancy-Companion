Nutrition Focus

This week, concentrate on folate, which is critical for your baby's neural tube development during early pregnancy.

Foods to Avoid

  • Raw or undercooked seafood - can expose you to harmful bacteria or parasites.
  • Unpasteurized dairy - may contain harmful bacteria.
  • Deli meats - pose risks of listeria infection if not heated properly.

Hydration Tip

Aim for at least 8-10 glasses of water each day to stay properly hydrated as your body goes through these changes.

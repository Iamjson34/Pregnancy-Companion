Your Baby's Development

This week, your baby is about the size of a lime! Their organs are continuing to form and mature, and they’re starting to develop fine hair called lanugo. Your little one is also practicing their swallowing skills and moving around more in the amniotic fluid.

Mom's Symptoms

  • Nausea: Try eating smaller, more frequent meals throughout the day to help manage nausea.
  • Fatigue: Make sure to carve out some time for rest, and don’t hesitate to ask for help when you need it!
  • Mood swings: Practice mindfulness or gentle yoga to help stabilize your emotions during this time of change.

Weekly Tip

Take 10-15 minutes daily for deep breathing or meditation to promote relaxation and emotional well-being.

Nutrition Focus

Folate is key this week for preventing neural tube defects as your baby’s brain and spine develop.

Foods to Avoid

  • Raw fish, due to the risk of parasites.
  • Unpasteurized dairy products, as they can harbor harmful bacteria.
  • Deli meats unless heated, which can pose a risk of listeria.

Hydration Tip

Aim for at least 8-10 glasses of water daily to keep hydrated and support increased blood volume during pregnancy.

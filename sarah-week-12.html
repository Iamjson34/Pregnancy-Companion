Week 12 of Your Pregnancy, Sarah!

You have 28 weeks to go until your due date!

Your Baby's Development

Your baby is about the size of a lime and is beginning to develop features like eyelids and ears. Their facial muscles are also starting to form, allowing for tiny expressions. By the end of this week, the baby is fully formed and is entering a growth phase.

Mom's Symptoms

  • Nausea: Try eating small, frequent meals throughout the day to help manage nausea.
  • Fatigue: Take time for short naps during the day, and prioritize restful sleep at night.
  • Mood swings: Consider practicing deep breathing or gentle yoga to help balance your emotions.

Weekly Tip

This week, take a few moments each day to connect with your baby by gently talking or playing music.

Message for John

John, this week you can help by preparing some of Sarah's favorite meals to keep her nourished and happy. Consider joining her for a relaxing evening walk to encourage bonding and connection. Leave little notes of encouragement around the house to brighten her day and support her throughout this first pregnancy journey.

Affirmation

You are doing an incredible job, Sarah, and you are stronger than you know.

Nutrition Focus

Folate, which is crucial for preventing neural tube defects and supporting your baby's brain development.

Foods to Avoid

  • Raw fish (risk of parasites)
  • Unpasteurized dairy (risk of bacteria)
  • Deli meats (risk of Listeria)

Hydration Tip

Aim to drink at least 8-10 glasses of water daily to stay hydrated and support increased blood volume.

Nutrition Focus

Folate — it’s essential for fetal brain development and helps prevent neural tube defects.

Foods to Avoid

  • Raw or undercooked seafood — it may contain harmful bacteria.
  • Unpasteurized dairy products — they can carry bacteria that are unsafe.
  • High mercury fish like swordfish — they can affect fetal development.

Hydration Tip

Aim for at least 8-10 glasses of water daily; staying hydrated is vital for both you and your developing baby.

Exciting Times Ahead, Sarah! You're in Week 12!

Only 28 weeks to go until your due date!

Your Baby's Development

At 12 weeks, your baby is about the size of a lime! Their vital organs have formed and are starting to function, and tiny buds for future teeth are forming in their gums. Plus, your little one is beginning to develop reflexes, so they might even start to wiggle around a bit!

Your Symptoms

  • Nausea: Keep crackers or a light snack on hand to nibble on before getting out of bed.
  • Fatigue: Consider taking short naps during the day to help recharge your energy levels.
  • Breast Tenderness: Wear a supportive bra to help ease discomfort and provide comfort throughout the day.

Your Weekly Tip

Take some time for yourself this week, even if it's just a quiet moment with a good book or a warm bath.

For You, John

  • Help Sarah with organizing or discussing baby names; it could make for a fun evening!

Nutrition Focus

Folate, which is essential for your baby’s rapidly developing cells.

Foods to Avoid

  • Unpasteurized cheese - can carry harmful bacteria.
  • Raw or undercooked seafood - poses a risk of foodborne illness.
  • Certain fish high in mercury - can affect fetal development.

Hydration Tip

Aim for at least 8-10 glasses of water a day to stay hydrated, especially if you're experiencing nausea.

👶 Your Baby This Week

Lime

At 12 weeks, your baby is starting to develop their facial features, including tiny fingers and toes. Their limbs are growing longer, and they can now move them more freely. By the end of this week, your baby will be about the size of a lime!

What To Expect This Week 🤱

Nausea ⚠️

Try eating small, frequent meals and keep ginger tea handy to help soothe your stomach.

Fatigue ⚠️

Make sure to prioritize rest and consider short naps when possible to recharge.

Breast tenderness ⚠️

Invest in a comfortable, supportive bra to ease discomfort during this time.

💡 This Week's Tip

Take a moment each day for mindfulness, whether through meditation or simply enjoying a quiet cup of tea.

🥦 Nutrition Focus

Folic Acid - It's crucial this week for your baby's neural development.

⚠️ Foods to Avoid

Unpasteurized cheeses - they may carry bacteria harmful to pregnancy.
Raw or undercooked eggs - they can carry the risk of salmonella.
Certain fish high in mercury - like swordfish and shark, which can affect baby's development.

💧 Hydration Tip

Aim for at least 8-10 glasses of water a day to stay hydrated and help with fatigue.

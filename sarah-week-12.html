Your Baby

Your baby is about the size of a lime! Their organs are further developing, and the tiny fingers and toes are starting to form. The facial features are becoming more pronounced, and your baby's body is beginning to take shape.

Nutrition Focus

Folate, as it helps in the baby's neural tube development.

Foods to Avoid

  • Raw fish: risk of bacteria and parasites
  • Unpasteurized dairy: risk of listeria
  • Processed meat: risk of nitrates and additives

Hydration Tip

Aim for at least 8-10 cups of fluids daily to stay hydrated, especially with the increased blood volume during pregnancy.

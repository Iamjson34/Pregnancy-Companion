👶 Your Baby This Week

Raspberry

Your baby is starting to develop more distinct facial features, such as a nose and mouth. Their tiny arms and legs are also beginning to grow, moving from being paddles to having recognizable limbs. Internal organs are maturing rapidly as they prepare for life outside the womb.

What To Expect This Week 🤱

Fatigue

Take short naps during the day if you can, and try to rest whenever you feel tired.

Nausea

Eat smaller, more frequent meals to help manage nausea throughout the day.

Frequent Urination

Stay hydrated, but try to limit fluids before bedtime to help reduce nighttime trips to the bathroom.

💡 This Week's Tip

Consider practicing deep breathing or mindfulness techniques to help ease any stress you might be feeling.

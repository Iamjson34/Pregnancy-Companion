👶 Your Baby This Week

Blueberry

At 8 weeks, your baby is about the size of a blueberry. Their facial features are beginning to develop, including eyes and nostrils. Tiny limbs are forming and your baby is starting to move their arms and legs, although you won't be able to feel it just yet!

What To Expect This Week 🤱

Nausea

Try eating small, frequent meals and keep some crackers or ginger tea nearby to help settle your stomach.

⚠️
Fatigue

Take short naps during the day when possible and ensure you’re getting plenty of rest at night.

⚠️
Frequent Urination

Stay hydrated but consider limiting fluid intake closer to bedtime to reduce nighttime trips to the bathroom.

⚠️
💡

This Week's Tip

Focus on getting plenty of rest and listen to your body; it's working hard to support your baby.

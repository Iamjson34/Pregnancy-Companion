Your Baby This Week

This week, your baby is developing important organs, including the heart and brain. Tiny limbs are starting to form, and facial features are beginning to take shape. Your little one is also rapidly growing, increasing in size as they prepare for the upcoming weeks.

What To Expect This Week 🤱

Nausea ⚠️

Try small, frequent meals throughout the day to keep nausea at bay.

Fatigue ⚠️

Prioritize rest and short naps when needed to recharge.

Frequent urination ⚠️

Keep a water bottle handy but limit nighttime fluids to sleep better.

💡This Week's Tip

Take time to enjoy a calming activity this week, such as a gentle walk in nature or some light prenatal yoga.
